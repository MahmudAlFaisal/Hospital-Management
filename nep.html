NEPHROLOGY

The nephrology department excellently identifies all types of kidney related diseases along with managing and healing them. This department treats all sorts of kidney-related conditions, such as:

  • high blood pressure
  • genetic kidney diseases
  • pre-birth kidney diseases
  • nephritis and vasculitis
  • polycystic kidney disease
  • kidney failure at an early age
  • kidney failure in old age (dialysis and kidney transplant)
  • Surgery for kidney and urinary disease, lithotripsy for kidney stones and disease management and also men’s sterility problems are cured with the help of the urology ward.
  • second stage kidney disease
  • Lordosis
  • MCL injuries
  • Scoliosis
  • Slipped capital femoral epiphysis (SCFI)
  • Spinal tumor
  • Spondylolysis
  • Spondylolisthesis
  • Sports injuries
